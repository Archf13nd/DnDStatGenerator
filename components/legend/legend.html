Legend:

Health

1st level is equal to hit die + constitution modifier. Levels after roll + constitution modifier

AC:

Unarmoured is 10 + dex, armoured use armour stat + dex to max specified

Hit Modifier:

Dexterity or strength modifier + proficiency bonus if proficient

Spell DC:

8 + proficiency bonus + your spellcasting ability modifier

Passive Check:

10 + modifier for the check

Skill and saving throw proficiency:

If proficient add your proficiency bonus, double the bonus for expertise

Inititive:

Equal to your dexterity modifier
